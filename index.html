All About Me!

By Alessandro Gregori

About My Family:

I am the youngest in my immediate family. I have an older brother named Lorenzo, who is in college right now so I do not see him often. My parents names are Giovanni (Dad) and Nell (Mom).

I have an uncle, an aunt, and three cousins from my dad's side who live in Italy. I see them often, usually every year during the summer, when me, my brother, and my dad travel to Italy for a few weeks.

Sailing:

When I was less than six years old, my brother used to sail. Many times I would go to the ckub that he went to to watch him and be with him. Once I got old enough to sail myself, I started immediately. I started when I was seven years old, and continued sailing until I was twelve. When I was twelve I had gotten too big for the boat that I had been sailing, so for a month or two I stopped, until I joined another program with a larger boat, that I am doing still now. Some notable things I accomplished while sailing is winning a few regattas (competitions for sailing) and even competing once when I was nine at the U.S youth championships.

Skiing:

Every winter except for the past three years, I went to Utah for a week to ski and see some extended family who live there. it had always been something I enjoyed extremely, because the feeling of being alone on top of a mountain going high speed is like nothing I had never experienced anywhere else. Unfortunately between Covid and other reasons I have not been able to ski recently and I am extremely sad about that.

Climbing:

I rarely go climbing, but when I do, I enjoy it very much. I sometimes go to a climbing gym which is about an hour drive from my house that has several different walls, but because of Covid, I havent gone recently. Back when I went, I considered myself decent at it, as I was able to do basically all of the vertical walls, and even some of the ones past ninety-degrees, but I was unable to do any of the expert walls.

Playing VideoGames:

Every since my brother started playing video games when I was little, I became enamored with them. The moment my mom let me get a console, I got one, and begun to play with my friends almost every day. When I turned thirteen, I got a gaming laptop, and begun to start gaming competitevely, and I actually got quite good at it. Recently there haven't been any good games, but my all time favorites have to be Titanfall 2 and Cod: Modern Warfare 2019.
